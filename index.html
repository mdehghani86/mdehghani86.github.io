Research Topic
Simulation, Optimization, and AI-Enabled Models for CAR T-Cell Therapy Supply Chain
About the Research
CAR-T cell therapy is an advanced form of personalized cancer treatment in which a patient's own immune cells are engineered to recognize and destroy cancer cells. Because each treatment is uniquely produced for an individual patient, the supply chain is highly complex and time sensitive, involving patient scheduling, manufacturing, and delivery coordination. Negar's research aims to develop and evaluate multiple solution approaches, based on simulation, optimization, and AI-enabled models, to better manage uncertainties in patient demand, processing time, and transportation. Her work seeks to identify cost-effective and reliable strategies for improving the overall efficiency and resilience of CAR-T cell therapy delivery.
Research Topic
Elicitron: AI Agent-Based Simulation Framework for Design Requirements Elicitation
About the Research
A lightweight framework that uses AI agents to simulate user interviews and generate product requirements automatically. It helps teams explore user needs, uncover hidden pain points, and speed up early-stage product discovery. The framework leverages Large Language Models to create diverse simulated users for comprehensive requirements gathering.
Research Topic
Agentic AI Applications in Finance
About the Research
This research explores the application of autonomous AI agents in financial systems. The work investigates how intelligent agents can automate complex financial tasks, make data-driven decisions, and optimize financial operations while maintaining transparency and compliance.
