IE5353

Storytelling with Data

Learn to transform complex data into compelling narratives that drive decisions and inspire action through effective visualization and communication techniques.

Fall & Spring
4 Credits
Graduate Level

Course Overview

This course focuses on the art and science of communicating data insights through compelling narratives. Students will learn to craft data stories that resonate with diverse audiences, from technical teams to executive leadership. The curriculum combines principles of visual design, cognitive psychology, and narrative structure.


Through hands-on projects, students will develop expertise in choosing the right visualizations, eliminating clutter, focusing attention, and building narratives that lead to actionable insights. The course draws heavily from industry best practices and real-world case studies.

Learning Objectives

1
Understand the principles of effective data communication and visual perception
2
Choose appropriate chart types and visualization techniques for different data types
3
Apply decluttering techniques to focus audience attention on key insights
4
Build compelling narrative structures that guide audiences through data
5
Create executive dashboards and presentations that drive business decisions
