Prof. Dehghani Receives DAIS Data Analytics Teaching Award

May 2025 | IISE Annual Conference & Expo

Congratulations to Associate Teaching Professor Mohammad Dehghani for receiving the Data Analytics & Information Systems (DAIS) Data Analytics Teaching Award!

The award is given by the DAIS division of the Institute of Industrial and Systems Engineers (IISE), the premier national society for industrial engineering. The award recognizes individual faculty for sustained performance of excellence in teaching DAIS-related courses.

Prof. Dehghani was honored for this award at the IISE Annual 2025 Conference & Expo. His dedication to teaching data analytics, simulation, and applied AI courses has made a significant impact on students in the Mechanical and Industrial Engineering department at Northeastern University.

This recognition highlights the importance of quality instruction in preparing the next generation of industrial engineers with strong data analytics and information systems skills.
